Install RTCW.
Download iortcw1.51c-win-x64
Drop the content into the RTCW folder.
Replace the WolfSP .exe by ioWolfSP.x64.exe for full steam integration.
(Optional) Replace the .exe shortcut icon by a fancier one.
Paste in the /Main folder the dedicated autoexec and config files.
